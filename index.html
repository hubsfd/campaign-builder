STEP 1

Campaign Setup

Name your campaign and set your focus before you start building your lead list. A well-defined campaign targets a specific industry and role so your outreach feels relevant, not generic.

STEP 2

Leads & Pre-Call Research

Add up to 10 leads. For each one, record what you found in your research — a news item, a LinkedIn post, an award, a company announcement. Then identify 1-3 possible pains or challenges. This research feeds your SPIN prep in the next step.

STEP 3

Outreach Prep

Review and edit your outreach scripts for each lead. These are pre-populated from your research notes — customize them before you send. Your only goal is to earn a first conversation.

STEP 4

Cadence Commitment

This is your commitment to your campaign. Block these dates on your calendar now — before you close this tool. Consistent follow-through on a 14-day cadence is what separates producers who get meetings from producers who get ghosted. Your campaign isn't real until the calendar blocks exist.

Enter your planned outreach date for each touchpoint and check which channels you'll use. Track your actual outcomes in CORE.

STEP 5

Review & Export

Review your complete campaign plan below. When you're ready, download it as a PDF to share with your coach or keep as a reference during your outreach.
